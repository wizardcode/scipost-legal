How It Works

  • Authorize your TikTok account via secure OAuth 2.0 login.
  • Select videos and add descriptions with hashtags.
  • SciPost uploads your videos directly to TikTok through the official API.
  • Track upload progress and publish status in real time.

Data & Privacy

SciPost only accesses the minimum data required: your TikTok profile info and the videos you choose to upload. All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not store, share, or sell any of your data. You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings.
