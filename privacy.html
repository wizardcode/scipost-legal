Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

SciPost ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our video publishing tool.

2. Information We Collect

When you use SciPost, we may collect the following information through TikTok's official API:

  • TikTok Account Information: Your TikTok username, display name, and profile avatar, as provided by TikTok during the OAuth authorization process.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to interact with TikTok on your behalf.
  • Video Content: Videos you choose to upload through the Service. These are transmitted directly to TikTok's servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your session.
  • To upload and publish video content to TikTok on your behalf.
  • To display your account information within the Service interface.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not store your videos on our servers. Videos are uploaded directly to TikTok.
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to any third parties.
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with TikTok via the official TikTok Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's Terms of Service and TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your authentication tokens only for as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may revoke access and delete your data at any time by:

  • Removing the app authorization from your TikTok account settings.
  • Deleting the local token file from your device.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
